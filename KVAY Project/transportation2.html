Korean Transportation

1. Seoul Metro

Seoul Metro is the top 4 metro system in the world. Using Seoul metro, you can go everywhere, even the airport. Experience the one of the best metro systems in the world.

2. Public Bus

So many lines of buses developed in Korea. You can go wherever you want by taking a bus, espcailly in Seoul.

3. Express Trains

KTX and SRT is the most popular and fastest express trains in Korea. People use train a lot to go to Busan and other southern regions.

4. Planes

Taking an airplane is the fastest way to go to beautiful Jeju island. Take the plane from Gimpo International Airport!
