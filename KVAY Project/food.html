Foods You MUST Eat in Korea

EXCHANGE (USD=>KRW)

1. Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a traditional Korean rice dish with various ingredients(vegetables, meats) in it. Enjoy the taste of Korea!

2. Korean Soups and Stews(Kimchi Stew, Doenjang Soup, Army Stew, etc)

Korean soup, soup, soup, and more soup! There are so many kinds of Korean soup. Find out your favorite.

3. Bulgogi

Bulgogi is one of the most popular traditional Korean meat dish. It is marinated slices of beef or pork grilled on a barbecue.

4. Samgyetang

Samgyetang is a hot chicken stew with lots of healthy vegetables in it. Korean usually eat this at summer.

5. Samgyeopsal

Basically, Samgyeopsal is a pork belly, but Koreans have special ways to eat them! See how's Korean barbecue different from anohter!

6. Korean Street Food(Hoetteok, Egg bread, etc)

There are abundance of street food in Korea. Go to any popular hangout area in Seoul and try some.
Myeongdong Main Street, Insadong Main Street, Hongdae are the most popular spaces.

7. Tteokbokki

Tteokbokki is a combination of ricecake, fishcake, and several vegetables with a spicy sauce.
Koreans usually eat them with sundae(Korean black pudding) or fried things.

8. Pajeon

Pajeon is Korean style pancake made with flour dough mixed with scallions, beef, fish, and other various ingredients

10. Spicy Instant Ramen with Slice Cheese

Korea has one of the best instant ramen in the world!
Adding a slice cheese on a ramen is one of popular methods how Koreans eat ramen.
